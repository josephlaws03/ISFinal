Here are some of the pros and cons of wind energy:

  1. Pros
    • Draws on a renewable resource (wind)
    • Extremely efficient in that it does not produce any waste
    • Can produce on average more than 6 million kWh in a year - enough to power 1500 average households
  1. Cons
    • Turbines pose a threat to wildlife such as birds and bats
    • Energy is unpredictable in the sense that if there were no wind, there would be no power
    • Turbines have a very expensive upfront cost
